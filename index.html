Welcome

Our web app is designed to help children with autism to do some of the everyday thing they will encounter. We provide step by step information on to complete each task or provide information as a reminder, hope it will help.

To start choose the desired everyday task on the left side of the screen and lets get started!

To start press on the menu button it will show you the everyday tasks, choose the one you are currently doing and lets get started!

About Autism

Around 700,000 people in the UK are on the autism spectrum. Together with their families, this means autism is a part of daily life for 2.8 million people.

You are not alone.
